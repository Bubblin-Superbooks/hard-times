Upon my word and honour I seem to be fated, and destined, and ordained, to live in the midst of things that I am never to hear the last of.  It really is a most extraordinary circumstance that it appears as if I never was to hear the last of anything!’

At about this point, Mr. Gradgrind’s eye would fall upon her; and under the influence of that wintry piece of fact, she would become torpid again.
